MEMO HAUS

FROM ARCHIVE TO PARTICIPATORY EXPERIENCE

Your memory belongs here too.

A shared, growing archive of Wolfsburg, built from the photos, voices, and stories of the people who actually live here. Scan the code, add yours, and step inside everyone else's.

01

Scan the code

Point your phone at the QR code on the pavilion. No app to install, it opens straight in your browser.

02

Share a memory

Upload a photo, place it on the map, and write what you remember. Add a voice note or a clip of music if you like.

03

Watch it come to life

Your photo is rebuilt into a 3D scene of drifting particles and joins the shared field on the pavilion's display.

04

Explore everyone's

Move through the shared map with your phone as a remote. Linger near a memory and it opens itself, story, sound and all.

A participatory memory archive, built with the people of Wolfsburg
